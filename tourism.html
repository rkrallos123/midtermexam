How to get here...

The nearest airport to Oroquieta City is the Labo Airport, also known as Ozamiz Airport. Flight time from Manila to Ozamiz Airport is approximately one hour. And Flight time from Cebu to Ozamiz Airport is approximately 45 minutes to one hour. From Ozamiz Airport, get a pedicab or tricycle going to Ozamiz Agora Bus Terminal. At Ozamiz Agora Bus Terminal you can find buses going to Oroquieta City. Travel time from Ozamiz City to Oroquieta is approximately 45 minutes to one hour. From Manila or Cebu, you can take a boat going to Ozamiz City Seaport, and then take a bus from Ozamiz to Oroquieta City. Or you can also take a boat going to Plaridel, Misamis Occidental Seaport via boat from Cebu City.
